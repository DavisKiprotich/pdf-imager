# PDF Imager — App Store Optimization (ASO) Strategy

This document outlines the optimized metadata, keyword strategy, and localized assets for the PDF Imager production release.

---

## 1. Store Identity (English - Primary)

- **Optimized Title:** PDF Imager — PDF to Image & Word
- **Subtitle (iOS):** Professional PDF conversion for images and docs.
- **Short Description (Android):** Fast PDF converter for images, Word documents, and text extraction.

### Core Description:
"The ultimate tool for your document workflow. Convert images to PDF, PDF to Microsoft Word, and extract text instantly with professional quality. Designed for speed, privacy, and ease of use across all your devices."

## 3. Keyword Strategy (100 Characters)

**Target Keywords:**
`pdf converter,image to pdf,pdf to word,jpg to pdf,word to pdf,extract text,ocr,batch converter,pdf scanner`

---

## 4. Visual Strategy (Screenshots & Graphics)

1. **Screen 1 (The Hook):** "Convert anything to PDF". Show the main dashboard with Image-to-PDF active.
2. **Screen 2 (The Key Feature):** "PDF to Word in Seconds". Show a document flowing from PDF into a Word layout.
3. **Screen 3 (History & Privacy):** "Your Files, Your Privacy". Highlight the History tab and local storage.
4. **Screen 4 (Multilingual):** Show the language selector with 9 flags. "Used Globally in 9 Languages".
5. **Screen 5 (Premium):** "Unlimited Conversions". Show the polished subscription cards.
